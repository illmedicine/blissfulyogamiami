The Heart Behind the Practice

Meet Paola Villegas

Yoga teacher. Birth doula. HypnoBirthing educator. Moon child. A warm soul dedicated to holding sacred space for transformation.

My Journey

From the Mat to the Birthing Room

My path into yoga and birth work wasn't linear — it was guided by intuition, the stars, and a deep calling to serve women during their most transformative chapter. Over a decade ago, I stepped onto my first yoga mat searching for peace. What I found was purpose.

"I believe every woman carries within her the ancient wisdom of birth. My role is simply to help her remember."

What began as a personal practice quickly became my life's mission. I trained in Vinyasa yoga, then specialized in prenatal instruction as I witnessed how profoundly yoga could prepare women — body, mind, and spirit — for birth. My certifications in HypnoBirthing and my work as a birth doula followed naturally, each one deepening my ability to hold space for the sacred.
