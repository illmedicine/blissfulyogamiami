Frequently Asked Questions

Is the consultation really free?

Yes! The initial 5-minute video consultation is completely free with no obligation. It's Paola's way of understanding your needs before recommending services.

Will my consultation be recorded?

Only with your explicit consent. Before recording begins, you'll be asked via a consent prompt. Recorded sessions may be shared on the site's testimonials section. You can decline recording and the consultation continues normally.

What do I need for the video call?

A device with a camera and microphone (laptop, tablet, or phone). A stable internet connection. We recommend using Chrome or Safari for the best experience.

How quickly will Paola respond to my booking?

Paola reviews appointment requests within 24 hours and accepts based on her schedule and availability. You'll receive a confirmation or alternative time suggestion via email.

📹 Recording Consent
