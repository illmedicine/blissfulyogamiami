  • Emotional healing and postpartum support circles

🌙 HypnoBirthing Education

The Mongan Method of HypnoBirthing teaches you to release fear and embrace the natural process of birth through deep relaxation, visualization, and self-hypnosis techniques.


What you'll learn:

  • 5-session comprehensive course (12.5 hours total)
  • Deep relaxation and self-hypnosis for labor
  • Visualization and affirmation techniques
  • Partner participation — building your birth team
  • Evidence-based information for informed decisions
  • Practice audio recordings for home use

Birth Support

💜 Birth Doula Services

Continuous emotional, physical, and informational support before, during, and after your birth. Paola's presence as your doula means you'll never walk this path alone.


Package includes:

  • 2 prenatal visits to build trust and create your birth plan
  • On-call availability from 38 weeks
  • Continuous labor and birth support
  • Comfort measures: massage, positioning, breathing guidance
  • Partner support and inclusion throughout
  • 1 postpartum visit for processing and breastfeeding support
