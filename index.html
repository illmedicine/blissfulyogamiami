Meet Your Guide

Paola Villegas

At the heart of Blissful Yoga Miami is Paola Villegas — a dedicated Vinyasa yoga and prenatal yoga specialist, certified HypnoBirthing educator, and birth doula with over a decade of experience supporting women through one of life's most powerful transitions.

"My mission is to help mothers-to-be feel empowered, informed, and deeply supported — physically, emotionally, and spiritually."

Her work combines mindful movement, evidence-based childbirth education, and nurturing presence to prepare women and their partners for a confident, connected birth experience.
